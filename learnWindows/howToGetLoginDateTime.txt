How to Get Login Date Time (in Windows)
---------------------------------------

Assumptions:
 A) You are running Windows 7
 

Procedure
---------
 1. Open a DOS window by pressing <Start><Run>CMD
 2. In the DOS window, type-in this:
    CMD> quser
         USERNAME              SESSIONNAME        ID  STATE   IDLE TIME  LOGON TIME
         >johnsmith            console             1  Active      none   1/18/2016 10:37 AM
