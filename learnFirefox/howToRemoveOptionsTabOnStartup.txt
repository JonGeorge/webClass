How to Remove Options Tab on Startup
------------------------------------

Whenever I startup Firefox, I see the options tab.  To remove this:

Procedure
---------
 1. Pull Tools / Options
 2. Click on the "General" tab
 3. Look at your home page:
    https://www.google.com/?gws_rd=ssl|about:preferences
    
    Remove the "|about:preferences" from the url
