VI Tricks
---------


Create a .vimrc file like this and put it in your home directory

unix> vi ~/.vimrc


"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
" Filename:  .vimrc
" Author:    Adam Resnick
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

" Turn off syntax colors
:syntax off

" Do not highlight
:set nohls

" Make searches case insensitive
:set ignorecase

: Turn off indending when pasting in a vi window
:set paste
