How to Install the Adobe Flash Player on Centos
-----------------------------------------------
WARNING:  Flash will always be a source of security holes

Procedure
---------
 1. Startup Firefox

 2. In Firefox, pull Tools / Add-ons
    Search for flash
    *or*
    Go to https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=flash&platform=Linux&appver=59.0
    
 3. Click on "Download Flash and Video "
    *or*
    Go to https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/download-flash-and-video/?src=search
    
 4. Click "Add to Firefox"
    Click "Add"
    
 5. Verify it works
    a. Open Firefox
    b. Go to youtube.com
    c. See if you can open a video
