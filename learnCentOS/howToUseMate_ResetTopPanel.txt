Working with MATE:  How to Reset the Top Panel
-----------------------------------------------
This will reset the mate panels back to the default
WARNING:  You will lose any of your added items and customizations

Procedure
---------
 1. Reset the MATE panel
    unix> mate-panel --reset

 2. Re-customize the MATE panel to the way you like it
    -- Add a shortcut for Chrome, Intellij, Shutdown....
