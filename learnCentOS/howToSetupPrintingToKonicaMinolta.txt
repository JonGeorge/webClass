How to Setup Printing to Minolta Konica Printer
-----------------------------------------------

Procedures
----------
 1. Startup the Print Settings
    Pull System -> Administration -> Print Settings
    *or*
    unix> system-config-printer
    
 2. Press "Add"
    a. Enter the root password a few times
 
 3. In the "Select Device"
    a. Click "Enter URI"
       Enter device URI:  socket://10.2.4.23:9100
    b. Press Forward
    
 4. In the "Choose Driver"
    a. Select "Search for a printer driver to download"
    b. For the Make and model:  Konica Minolta C352
    c. Press Search
    d. Select Konica Minolta BizHub C352
    e. Press Forward
    f. Press Forward again
 
 5. In the "Installable Options"
    a. Use the defaults Press Forward
   
 6. In the "Describe Printer" popup
    a. Use the defaults and press "Apply"
    
 7. You will be prompted to print a test page
    a. Press "Print Test Page"
    b. Press OK
    c. Verify that the test page appeared
