How to Install the Sense Plugin for Chrome
------------------------------------------
Problem:  Google no longer lets users download the "Sense" plugin

Solution: I uploaded my google chrome extension to this github site
          Manually Transfer the files to your computer
          
Procedure
---------
 1. Download the zip file to your computer
    a. Open a browser
    b. Go to github.com/traderres
    c. Click on WebClass
    d. Click on lhjgkmllcaadmopgmanpapmpjgmfcfig.zip
    e. Click on Raw
       or
       Go to https://github.com/traderres/webClass/raw/master/learnCentOS/lhjgkmllcaadmopgmanpapmpjgmfcfig.zip
       
 2. Unzip the file to the appropriate directory on your computer
    On Windows:  Upload to here:  C:\Users\Adam\AppData\Local\Google\Chrome\User Data\Default\Extensions
    On Linux:    Upload to here:  ~/.config/google-chrome/Default/Extensions
    On Mac:      Upload to here:  ~/Library/Application Support/Google/Chrome/Default/Extensions/ or 
                                  ~/Library/Application Support/Google/Chrome/<Profile 1>/Extensions/

 3. Verify it works
    a. Startup Chrome
    b. Go to here:  chrome-extension://lhjgkmllcaadmopgmanpapmpjgmfcfig/index.html
       -- You should see the Sense plugin
