How to Disable the Samsung Hot Spot Timeout
-------------------------------------------
Problem:  On my Samsung s9 smartphone, the hot spot turns off after 30 minutes of inactivity
Solution: Turn off the timeout


Procedure
---------
 1. Pull the Settings
 2. Select Mobile Hot Spot
 3. Press "Details
 4. Press 3-dots in the upper right corner
 5. Select "Timeout settings"
 6. Select "Never timeout"
