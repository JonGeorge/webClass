How to Add Audio to a Video (using Corel VideoStudio)
-----------------------------------------------------


Procedures
----------
 1. Drag and drop an audio file into Corel VideoStudio
 2. Drag and drop that audio file fro mthe "Media" section to your audio track
 
 3. Mute all of your video segments  [so the music is the only thing you here]
    Select all of your video segments -> Right-click -> Mute
    
 4. Play your video segments with audio
    a. Press Control-A
    b. Press the Play button
