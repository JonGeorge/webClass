How To Get Info about Your Virtual Machine
------------------------------------------

Assumptions:
 A) Your vm is called "CentOS 6"
 
 
Procedure
---------
 1. Open a DOS window by pressing <Start><Run>CMD
 2. Within the window, type-in this:
    DOS> cd /d C:\Program Files\Oracle\VirtualBox
    DOS> VBoxManage.exe showvminfo "CentOS 6"
