How to Change your Jenkins Password
------------------------------------

Procedure
---------
 1. Connect to your Jenkins site
 2. Login as your username/password
 3. Click on your name [in the upper right corner
    Click on "Configure"
    *OR*
     Go to http://my.jenkins.server.com/me/configure
     
 4. Scroll down to the password boxes and set a new password
 5. Press Save
