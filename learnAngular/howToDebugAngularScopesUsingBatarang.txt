How to Debug Angular Scopes using the Batarang Chrome Extension
---------------------------------------------------------------

Assumptions:
 A) You have chrome installed
 B) You want to view your Scopes in an angular app
 
 
The Batarang Chrome Extension adds an additional AngularJS tab to the Inspector


Procedure
---------
 1. Download the batarang chrome extension
 
 2. Use chrome to view your angular page
 
 3. In Chrome follow these steps:
    a. Open the page of your angular page
    b. Press Ctrl-Shift-I to open the Inspector
    c. At the top, look for the AngularJS tab
    d. Click on the "Enable" checkbox
    e. Click on Models
       -- You should be able to see all scopes
