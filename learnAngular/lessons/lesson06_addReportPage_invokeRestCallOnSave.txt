Lesson:  Add Report Page:  Invoke REST Call on "Save"
-----------------------------------------------------
When the user clicks to "Add Report", we want to make a REST call



Procedure
---------
 1. Create a ReportService (that makes the REST calls)

 2. Inject the ReportService into the Add-Report Component

 3. Upon pressing save, call the ReportService.add()
    a. Creates a ReportDTO
    b. Makes a call to POST /api/reports/add
