How to Make an Image Transparent (using Paint.net)
--------------------------------------------------

Assumptions:
 A) You have paint.net installed
 

Procedure
---------
 1. Startup Paint.net
 
 2. Open the image
 
 3. Set the transparency to 25%
 
 4. Click on a section that you want to be transparent
 
 5. Pull Edit / Cut
